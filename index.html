FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've got answers.

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

No. Our free Starter plan is genuinely free, and the 14-day Growth trial doesn't require a card upfront.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your account settings. Changes prorate automatically.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Nova is SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR-compliant, and encrypts data at rest and in transit.

Do you offer student or nonprofit discounts?

Yes. Students get 50% off Growth, and verified nonprofits qualify for free Growth plans. Reach out and we'll set you up.

What kind of support is included?

Starter includes community support. Growth gets priority email with 24-hour response. Enterprise gets a dedicated account manager.
